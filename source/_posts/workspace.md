## oh-my-zsh
- [oh-my-zsh](https://github.com/robbyrussell/oh-my-zsh)  可以通过Plugins使用`Git`、`Brew`等功能
- [Plugins](https://github.com/robbyrussell/oh-my-zsh#plugins) 通过修改`~/.zshrc`文件来启用插件功能

```
plugins=(git bundler osx rake ruby)
```
